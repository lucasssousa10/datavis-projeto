Covid-19 immunization analysis


Since 2020, the covid-19 pandemic has affected the entire world population. Several health restrictions have been imposed, and there has been a drastic change in all humans' lifestyles. Several tools were developed to monitor the evolution of the pandemic.

This tool will allow an analysis of the vaccination process in the countries of the world. In addition, the tool presents information regarding the side effects caused by vaccines.
